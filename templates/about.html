All about Coffee

Coffee is primarily grown in the equatorial zone, coffee plants prefer fertile soil, mild temperatures rain and shaded sun. The two coffee species that people primarily drink are Arabica and Robusta. Arabica coffee is preferred by 70% globally while Robusta is only at 30%. Our dataset found that 98% prefer Arabica coffee over Robusta. Arabica coffee tends to be sweeter and be close to the taste of chocolate, while Robusta is said to taste like burnt oil or burnt rubber.
